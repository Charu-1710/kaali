About Kaali Foundation

Founded in 2021, Kaali Foundation is dedicated to creating awareness, spreading knowledge, and empowering communities by bridging the gap between welfare schemes, guidance, and opportunities.

Our Story

Kaali Foundation was born from a simple belief: that awareness is the first step toward empowerment. Many people remain unaware of the welfare schemes, career guidance, and support available to them. What began as a small local effort to spread knowledge has now evolved into a movement for change and empowerment.

Our passionate team of volunteers and professionals work tirelessly to provide guidance, connect individuals with opportunities, and create a support system that enables people to lead better and more informed lives.
